Every Second Counts

>

From The Bear

One of my favorite shows is The Bear in part because it's about food, drive, and Chicago - three great things in this world. When Richie is working at Alinea, there is this sign that gets a spotlight shot:

The phrase "Every Second Counts" is complicated, but many parts of it resonated with me, so I wanted to make my own version. I CADed up a simple design and brought it to life at MIT's makerspaces. It's made from painted laser-cut plywood, with a blue acrylic front panel with an acetal film diffuser, and backlit by LEDs. Simple, but very happy with how it turned out.
