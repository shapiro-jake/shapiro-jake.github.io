Glassblowing

MIT Glass Lab, 2020-Present

During my freshman year, I walked past a glass sale on campus and was mesmerized. Turns out, MIT has a hotshop on campus and I was lucky enough to get into the beginner class. Now, I help teach the beginner classes, help make pumpkins for the MIT Great Glass Pumpkin Patch, and make my own work. I love clean, solid colors with complementing highlights.
